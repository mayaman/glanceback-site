Glance Back Privacy Policy

Posted: July 29th, 2019

After you grant Glance Back permission to access the webcam, once a day at random it will take a photo and ask you to type what you are thinking about.

It’s important to note that all of the photos and thoughts are saved locally to your browser’s local storage. This means that they never leave your machine or are stored on any servers.

This is a collection shared purely between you and your computer. If you want the photos for yourself, you can choose to download them under the settings tab in the top right. You can also delete photos from the archive and choose whether the extension requires you to write a caption or not.

Learn more about the project at glanceback.info.
